RULES

OBJECTIVE

Be the first player to connect 4 of the marker colored disc in a row (either vertically, horizontally or diagonally)

HOW TO PLAY

1

Red goes first in the first game

2

Player must alternate turns, and only one disc can be dropped in each turn

3

The game ends when there is a 4-in-a-row or a stalemate

4

The starter of the previous game, goes second in the next game
